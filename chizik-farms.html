Chizik Farms and Services is a growing agricultural enterprise based in Abakaliki, Ebonyi State — focused on high-quality piggery farming and sustainable livestock production for individuals, businesses, and communities across the region.

We believe in clean, structured, and ethical farm operations that deliver premium-grade livestock and reliable agribusiness support. Every animal is raised with care. Every client is served with professionalism.
